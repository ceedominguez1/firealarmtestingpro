Torrance Fire Alarm Compliance FAQ

Who is the AHJ for fire alarm inspections in Torrance?

The Torrance Fire Department (TFD) Fire Prevention Bureau is the Authority Having Jurisdiction within Torrance city limits, enforcing NFPA 72 as adopted by the California Fire Code.

Do you handle industrial and manufacturing systems?

Yes. We routinely test addressable systems, beam detectors, air-sampling detection (VESDA), and interfaces to clean-agent and pre-action suppression systems that are common across Torrance's industrial corridor.

Can you coordinate inspections around production schedules?

Yes. We schedule around shift changes, planned downtime, and production windows so we can isolate notification appliances and test without disrupting operations or triggering false alarms.

What is the typical lead time in Torrance?

Most non-emergency inspections in Torrance are scheduled within 5–10 business days. Same-day and next-day emergency response is available for impairments and fire-watch coverage.
