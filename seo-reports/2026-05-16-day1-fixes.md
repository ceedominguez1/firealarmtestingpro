## Why these were prioritized
- **Schema is the single biggest GEO/AI-engine lever** — without it AI engines can't reliably extract your service area, services, hours, or FAQs. Adding it is a one-time fix with permanent compounding benefit.
- **Canonical + sitemap + robots** = baseline crawlability. Skipping these means Google has to guess.
- **OG / Twitter cards** = every share on LinkedIn / WhatsApp / X / Slack now shows a proper preview instead of a bare URL.
- **Pinned Lucide** = no more silent breakage if upstream changes; also long-cache TTL improves repeat-visit speed.
