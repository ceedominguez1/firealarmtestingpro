San Fernando Valley Fire Alarm Compliance FAQ

Which fire department is the AHJ in the SFV?

It depends on the city. Most SFV neighborhoods inside the City of Los Angeles (Van Nuys, Sherman Oaks, Studio City, Northridge, Encino, Reseda, Tarzana, Woodland Hills, Sylmar) fall under LAFD. Burbank Fire Department covers Burbank, Glendale Fire Department covers Glendale, and San Fernando Fire Department serves the City of San Fernando. We work fluently with all four.

Do studio facilities and post-production buildings have special requirements?

Yes. Many SFV studios, soundstages, and post-production facilities have specialized systems (clean agent suppression, pre-action sprinkler, smoke control). We test to NFPA 72, NFPA 75, and NFPA 2001 standards and schedule around production calendars.

Can you handle multi-site portfolios across the Valley?

Yes. We routinely manage multi-property inspection calendars for apartment portfolios, retail centers, and medical groups spanning multiple SFV cities — with a unified report bundle and a single compliance contact.

What is the lead time to schedule an SFV inspection?

Most non-emergency inspections in the SFV are scheduled within 5–10 business days. Emergency repair response is available with same-day or next-day arrival.
